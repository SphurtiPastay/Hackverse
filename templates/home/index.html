💥THE OBJECTIVES

Providing students the opportunity to work towards the sustainable development goals by identifying issues and offering solutions for real-world challenges

Giving students a platform to work in a team of like-minded students, thereby enhancing their skills and knowledge by sharing and collaborating

To test the analytical capability of the students of Lavasa campus

💥 HACKVERSE

HackVerse in three exciting rounds!

Phase 1: Prelims feature an online MCQ/MSQ quiz on Analytics, CHRIST University, and Sustainable Development goals.

Phase 2: Top teams proceed to Phase 2 for SDG Visualization, where they visually represent a sustainable goal.

Phase 3: Finale is an offline event with prototype building, mentorship, and evaluations.

Don't miss the chance to innovate and compete!
